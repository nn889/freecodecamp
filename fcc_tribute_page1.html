Kim Jun-myeon (Korean: 김준면; born May 22, 1991), better known by his stage name Suho (Korean: 수호, meaning "guardian"), is a South Korean singer, songwriter, and actor. He is the leader of the South Korean-Chinese boy group Exo and its sub-unit Exo-K. He debuted as a soloist on March 30, 2020 with the release of his EP, Self-Portrait. Outside of his musical career, Suho has also starred in various television dramas and movies such as One Way Trip (2016), The Universe's Star (2017), Rich Man (2018), Middle School Girl A (2018), and How Are U Bread (2020).
